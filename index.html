About the group

Theory that moves into the real world.

The Electromagnetics Group is part of the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Houston. Our work centers on antennas and computational electromagnetics, with an emphasis on both fundamental understanding and experimental validation.

Research

Core areas

A collaborative program spanning analytical methods, numerical modeling, and laboratory measurement.

01

Antenna Systems

Analysis, design, and experimental validation of antennas across microwave and millimeter-wave frequencies.

  • Microstrip antennas
  • Leaky-wave antennas
  • Dielectric resonators
02

Computational Electromagnetics

Accurate numerical methods for complex electromagnetic structures, supported by theory and high-performance computation.

  • Full-wave modeling
  • Numerical methods
  • Periodic structures
03

Waves & Engineered Materials

Wave phenomena in periodic, metamaterial, plasmonic, and other engineered electromagnetic environments.

  • Metamaterials
  • Plasmonics
  • Wave propagation
